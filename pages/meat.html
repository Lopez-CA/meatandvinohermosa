About Us

At the Hermosa Butcher shop, we are dedicated to providing our customers with high quality sources of protein and overall goodness. That includes carefully selected wine choices and the best of local breweries. Our commitment to customer satisfaction drives everything we do, from the sourcing of the best meat available to preparing and serving prime cuts of steak and marinated meats with care. We focus on connecting quality with flavor. Our goal is to bring Hermosa Beach a new source of sutainable nutritous products you can trust.
