Cabernet Sauvignon is a full-bodied red wine known for its high tannin content and flavors of dark fruit, such as blackcurrant, blackberry, and plum, as well as notes of vanilla, oak, and spice from aging in oak barrels. It is a popular varietal that is grown in many wine regions around the world.

Cabernet Sauvignon pairs well with hearty, flavorful dishes such as grilled or roasted red meats (beef, lamb), strong cheeses, and dishes with rich tomato-based sauces. It can also pair well with dark chocolate desserts.
