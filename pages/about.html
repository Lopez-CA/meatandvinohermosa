We are a local butcher that specializes in fresh handmade sausages and fine cuts of meat. At Hermosa Butcher Shop our mission is to blend community, innovation and tradition. Our community is the heart of our business. With every cut, every conversation, and every meal we help create, we hope to honor the rich heritage of butchery while looking to the future. We don't consider ourselves's to be a finished product yet. However, we are working hard to get there. Our goal is develop classic dishes using traditional processes to create something that is just a little bit better than the rest.
